Thermal Time (oC Days)

Thermal time is used to define the time based on accumulated temperature change.

“Leaves are assumed to grow and senesce at a rate determined by the thermal time integral of the mean daily temperature(Thermal Time) above a base temperature of 1oC” (Porter 1984). We use a base temperature of 0oC within the Ewert Model.
